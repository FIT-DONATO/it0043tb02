TANDEMAUS

Tandemaus is a Pokémon comprising two separate mouse-like rodent Pokémon which work together as one, much like Exeggcute or Falinks.

The pair that forms Tandemaus sticks together no matter what. They exhibit great teamwork: they share any food they find equally within the pair, and they build their nest together using materials they've gathered with their incisors.


Giving your partner a Tandemaus isn't just a symbol that you like them. It shows that you want to build something with them. These are two mice that, no matter what, stand by and support each other, and you're going to do the same.
